Robotics and AI

Artificial intelligence is where systems emulate the human mind to learn, solve problems and make decisions on the fly, without needing the instructions specifically programmed.

AI & Robotics

AI is becoming increasingly integral to the development of robotics, allowing robots to perform complex tasks that require learning, decision-making, and interaction with humans and the environment. Here are some key ways AI enhances robotics: Autonomy: AI allows robots to function autonomously, making decisions in real time. This is particularly important in environments where pre-programming every action would be impractical (e.g., self-driving cars or warehouse robots). Computer Vision: AI-powered computer vision enables robots to see and understand their environment, identify objects, and make sense of visual data. This is essential for tasks like object manipulation, navigation, and inspection.
